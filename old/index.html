PROMETHEUS V
Prometheus is the annual Technical Fest of IECSE. This is the fifth time the fest will be organized by the club. The technical extravaganza will begin on the 1st of April and will continue till the 3rd. We have planned eight events for Prometheus 5.0 which includes both technical and non technical competitions. In the previous years we have seen participation in mass numbers in all events and we expect an even greater turn up this time.

It's 'Gods amongst men' this time. So as we say,
Let the games begin!
EVENTS
HawkEye
HawkEye is an online scavenging hunt where you match wits against hundreds of participants solving cryptic puzzles and collecting small but vital clues to advance through the levels.
Pandoras Bugs
Track bugs all the way! A team event, where the first round comprises tracking bugs in short code snippets, with certain restrictions. Find them, squash them, squash them again.
Dawn of Justice
After a screening test is done, Different teams will be alloted different superheroes which have special powers. Using those powers to their advantage, teams need to code to bag the winning position.
Online Coding
Enjoyed participating in competitions on Hackerrank and CodeChef? Immerse yourself in mind boggleing coding questions on an online judge for 3 days straight.
Bamboozled
MIT's first ever game show! Again. This time, bigger, better, crazier. Come join the madness and be part of an event you'll never forget.
Artem
Love designing and drawing? Test your graphic design skills in this 2-round event.
Paper Presentation
he paper presentation is an opportunity to every participant to showcase an idea that can change it's current perception. There is a chance for every idea to be showcased and presented to the world.
PORT:80
First ever Web Dev event. Think you can build front ends and backends? Come, prove your skills and become a god.
